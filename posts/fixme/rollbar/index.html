Instalando rollbax

  • https://github.com/elixir-addicts/rollbax

Adicionar rollbax à lista de dependência em ~/git/opa/mix.exs

1defp deps() do 
2  # ...
3    {:rollbax, "~> 0.6"}
4  # ...
5end

Adicionar rollbax à lista de aplicações em ~/git/opa/mix.exs

 1  def application do
 2    [
 3      mod: {Opa, []},
 4      applications: [
 5        :phoenix, :phoenix_pubsub, :phoenix_html, :cowboy, :logger, :gettext, :logger_file_backend,
 6        :phoenix_ecto, :postgrex, :timex, :timex_ecto, :httpoison, :bcrypt, :tirexs, :erlware_commons,
 7        :earmark, :erlcloud, :mime, :secure_random, :scrivener_ecto, :rollbax
 8      ]
 9    ]
10  end

Atualizar dependências

1mix deps.get

Configurar ~/git/opa/config/config.exs

1config :rollbax,
2  access_token: "357852747b8f454d98d5a68ac57baf9d",
3  environment: "production"

Configurar o logger para usar rollbax

 1# Configures Elixir's Logger
 2config :logger, :console,
 3  format: "$date::$time $metadata[$level] $message\n",
 4  metadata: [:request_id]
 5
 6# We register Rollbax.Logger as a Logger backend.
 7config :logger,
 8  backends: [Rollbax.Logger]
 9
10# We configure the Rollbax.Logger backend.
11config :logger, Rollbax.Logger,
12  level: :error
