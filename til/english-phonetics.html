Some tips for better pronunciation:

Vowel

  • a, e, i, o and u
  • 12 sounds
IPA vowel duration examples portuguese sound
ɑ: a large car, far a aberta
æ a short cat é nasal
ʌ a short sun, up a

e e short pet, bed e
ə e short teacher a (schwa)
i: i large sheep i
I i short ship, fit i virando é
ɔ: o large door ó
ɒ o short on ó
u: u large shoot u
ʊ u short good u
